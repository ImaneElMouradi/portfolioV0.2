About Me

I’m Imane El Mouradi, 21 years old and currently a 2nd year student in Mohammadia School of Engineering in Rabat-Agdal Morocco. I’ve been passionate about technology especially computer science for as long as I can remember.

Technology is what gave me the opportunity to play video games, to watch my favorite shows, to learn more about every aspect of life with a simple click etc.… That’s why I’ve always wanted to be apart of this movement that could change a lot of people’s life, making it easier and better.

In the past few years, I started to learn how to write algorithms and programs to solve problems. Then, I learned how to translate those words and instructions into functional, simple to use, programs. This ability to solve problems with a few lines of code is what made me fall in love with this field.

Now, I want to create different applications (desktop, mobile or web) to help users access different type of data easily and efficiently, with a beautiful and functional design.

“You can do anything as long as you have the passion, the drive, the focus, and the support.” —Sabrina Bryan

What I do

Design UI/UX

In my opinion, well designing an application (be it a mobile, a desktop or a web application) is the most important part.

In fact, your deisgn is what impacts how your audience perceives your brand. The impression you make on them can either get them to remain on your page and learn about your business or leave your page and turn to a competitor.

That's why I've been interested inlearning more about this aspect of current applications in order to create something beautiful, sustainable, accessible and ergonomic.


Tools: Photoshop, Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator.

Front End

"Front-end web development is the practice of converting data to a graphical interface, through the use of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, so that users can view and interact with that data."

Since designing an application wasn't enough for me, I decided to enter the realm of development starting with front end web development.

I always found it interesting to translate designs and pictures to a functional static web page that could be deployed and used by many users all around the world.


Tools: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, ReactJS.

Back End

Design and Front-end web development enables to create static web applications only, it wasn't possible to manipulate databases or servers.

At first, I started using different free APIs to gather information such as APIs about the weather, food, github repositories etc... (You can find examples in my work below).

Eventually, I realised that it wasn't enough and that it was too restrictive. That's mainly the reason why I decided to learn more about backend and create my own RESTful APIs.


Tools: NodeJS, ExpressJS, MongoDB, MongoDB Atlas, Spring Boot, MySQL, Asp.NET, SQL Server.
